2022 University Surveys (with $100 Prezzy cards to be won!) Check your University email! You may be one of 20,000 randomly selected students asked to complete either the University’s 2022 Learning and Teaching Survey or the 2022 Research on Academic Integrity in New Zealand (RAINZ) Survey. Have your say and go into the draw for 1 of 60 $100 Prezzy vouchers. Each survey takes 10-15 minutes to complete. Both surveys will help the University make important decisions. They are voluntary and confidential, and are your chance to have your say, give feedback and suggest ways to improve the student experience. For past years’ Learning & Teaching survey results and further information please see www.auckland.ac.nz/surveyLinks to an external site.
